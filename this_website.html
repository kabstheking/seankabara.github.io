About

Creating a website like this is actually not too difficult. I just looked it up online and found this basic templates website, HTML5 UP and followed a youtube tutorial on how to get it set up. From there, some messing around and figuring out how HTML works can get you pretty far.

I would encourage any college student, especially engineering students to make a portfolio website like this. It gives you a real chance to expand upon what is on your resume and let recruiters and hiring managers know what you’re really about. Definitely reach out using my contact info at the bottom of this page if you have questions about getting started, I’d be happy to help out.
